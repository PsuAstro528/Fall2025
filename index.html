High-Performance Scientific Computing for Astrophysics

Astro 528 (Fall 2025)

High-Performance Scientific Computing for Astrophysics will combine class discussion and programming exercises to train students in the use of modern computing hardware and programming strategies for application to astronomy and astrophysics research.
